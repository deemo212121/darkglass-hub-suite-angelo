DARKGLASS HUB SUITE - PROGRESS UPDATE
Covering: July 9-10, 2026


JULY 9 - TRUCK STOCK APPROVALS + NOTIFICATION FIXES
-----------------------------------------------------
- Finished wiring up the Jotform notification bell icon (was left "in
  progress" from July 8) - clicking a Jotform notification now actually
  takes you to the HR Dashboard instead of doing nothing.
- Truck Stock pull-request approval is now limited to Admin, Superadmin,
  and Parts Manager only - Claims Manager and other generic Manager
  roles can no longer see or act on approval requests.
- Fixed a real bug where a Truck Stock pull request submitted by an
  Admin would auto-approve itself and skip Parts Manager review
  entirely. Every request now requires explicit approval, no matter who
  submitted it. Manually corrected the one part record that had gotten
  stuck in the wrong status because of this.
- Approve/Reject notifications sent back to the requester are now clear
  and specific ("Part X for Ticket Y is Approved/Rejected"), and a
  rejection can include the Parts Manager's reason so the requester
  knows what to do next.
- Added a "Rejected" tab to Truck Stock Requests (next to Pending and
  Approved) showing who rejected each request, when, and why.
- Truck Stock notifications now click straight through to the exact
  request that triggered them - the page scrolls to and highlights that
  specific row instead of just opening a generic tab.


JULY 10 - WORK CALENDAR REBUILD, LIGHT MODE FIXES, PAYROLL ACCURACY
---------------------------------------------------------------------
- Rebuilt the Work Calendar (Monthly) page to run on real scheduled
  tickets instead of randomly-generated placeholder data. "Engineer" is
  now "Technician," and the technician list is pulled from the real
  company roster instead of a made-up list.
- Fixed the month picker - Next/Previous now reliably move one full
  month at a time. What looked like "jumping to a random date" was
  actually the old fake data reshuffling which tickets landed on which
  day every time the month changed; real ticket dates don't do that.
- Fixed several spots on the Work Calendar page (panel background,
  month arrows, view toggle buttons, banner text) that stayed dark even
  after switching the app to light mode.
- Looked into why the Payroll tab was showing $0 for everyone: confirmed
  it's a live calculation, not something gated behind "Generate
  Payroll." It was showing $0 because payroll had just been generated
  through July 8, so the live window was a single, mostly-empty day
  (July 9). It will fill in on its own as new clock-ins/outs come in.
  Separately flagged that most employees showing $0.00 pay rate simply
  don't have an hourly rate set up yet - a data gap, not a bug.
- Found and fixed a real payroll-accuracy bug: a Time Correction request
  had its clock-in AM/PM flipped (8:24 AM saved as 8:24 PM), which
  silently zeroed out that whole day's pay with no warning to anyone.
  Both submitting and approving a Time Correction now block obviously
  wrong times (check-out before check-in) instead of accepting them
  silently. Corrected the one affected timecard record.
